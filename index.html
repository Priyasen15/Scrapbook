One last thing

A Letter From My Heart

So, I hope I made you remember every beautiful moment we have shared together. And now comes the final part.

You have no idea how happy and grateful I am to have a friend like you in my life, Shanmugha.

I am kind of an avoidant person. Your constant check-ups, your care, and the way you never let go of me are the reasons this friendship is still so beautiful even after five years.

I have realized that I usually get exhausted with relationships and slowly distance myself from people. I never really felt a bond as strong as ours in the past or even now in hostel.

But with you, it is only because of YOU that our friendship still exists today.

Because of you, I proudly get to say, "I have a best friend, and that's YOU." I really owe you a lot for never letting me quietly disappear from your life and for making this friendship so beautiful.

I am also grateful that because of you I got another lovely family.

To our cool Vidhya Aunty, who has always supported me since our JEE days, and to our lovable Aachi, whose warm smile, hugs, and welcome always make me feel at home whenever I visit. Those little moments mean more than you know.

I hope this bond stays with us till the very end. No matter where life takes us, no matter how busy life becomes, I hope we always remain BRO.

Thank you for staying. For caring. For understanding me. For choosing this friendship.

Happy 20th Birthday once again, Bro.

Love,
